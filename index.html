About TravelUSA

As an award winning travel company, here at TravelUSA, we aim to help you create the adventure of a lifetime. We don't believe that one size fits all, and our travel experts create bespoke package deals which specifically caters to your needs. Whether you want to enjoy the glorious landscape and national parks, stay in 4* hotels or rustik motels, check out baseball games and eat at the best local restaurants, we have the package for you. Take a look at just some of the amazing packages we have available. If you don't see a deal that's for you, get in touch and we will create a package that's just right for you

Why choose us?
We provide bespoke holiday packages for our customers. We understand that everyone is different so we want you to tell us what you want out of your trip, and we will do the rest. Whether you're travelling alone, with young children or enjoying your retirement, we can help!
How much will it cost you?
We provide competetive prices based upon the duration of your holiday and what you want to do while you're away. You will be assigned your very own travel expert who you will assist you with all that you need, up until the point of take off. Complete the 'contact us' form below and tell us more.
What services do we provide?
We provide everything from your airport transportation to hotel reservations. We can also handle your ESTA visa, book tickets to Disney world theme parks at a discounted price, hire rental cars and book helicopter rides over New York City and the Grand Canyon.
